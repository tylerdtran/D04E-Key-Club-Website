Meet our 2020-2021 Lieutenant Governor

Vincent Vu

Message to Division 4 East: What’s splashing, D04E? My name is Vincent Vu, and I proudly serve Division 4 East as their Lieutenant Governor! I can vividly recall the moment I was elected Lieutenant Governor Elect at Conclave on December 14, 2019. Although I was unsure if I was qualified enough to serve our division, I knew I had the ability to make a difference. After witnessing the growth of our division during the 2019-2020 term, I truly wanted to take our division to new heights. I noticed that from the very beginning of my Key Club journey that although we were small in size, we had the biggest hearts, and I wanted to change that. I wanted to give our division the reputation of being humongous, like other divisions within our Region. Along with that, I wanted to break records set by the previous term and serve 5,500 hours and raise $5,500 for PTP. However, these are all just numbers to me. The most important thing to me during this term is to develop strong bonds with each of my members that I serve. I want to be the Lieutenant Governor that is friends with each of his members so that each person that attends an event has someone to talk to. I know that sometimes it may be difficult to make friends, but I promise to give my whole heart to making your time in Key Club memorable. We’re gonna definitely break records and make history at the rate that we’re going right now. As I said at Conclave, let’s make some big GAINS this term!
