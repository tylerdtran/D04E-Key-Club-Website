Current Service Events

Color-A-Smile

Color-A-Smile is a non-profit organization seeking to bring happiness and smiles through cheerful drawings to senior citizens, our troops overseas, and anyone in need of a smile.

Instructions:

  1. Click the Image to visit the Color-A-Smile Website
  2. Print a Color-In Page provided through the website
  3. Color the page using crayons or markers (NO GLITTER)
  4. Sign the drawing with your First Name and Age and mail the drawings to the location provided: Color A Smile PO Box 1516 Morristown NJ 07962-1516
  5. Record your hours via the google form via our linktree.

Charity Miles

Charity Miles is an app that records your distance exercised and donates money to your selected charity based on the amount of distance you walk, run or bike. Charity Miles is a great way to get out and exercise while also contributing to charity.

Instructions:

  1. Download and create an account for the Charity Miles App
  2. Select Key Club's preferred Charities March of Dimes or UNICEF as your charity of choice
  3. Join your Home Club's Charity Miles Team
  4. Record your miles!!
  5. Log your Service Hours via the service hour log via our linktree

Free Rice

Free Rice is an app that allows you to prevent world hunger simply by answering trivia questions. For every 1 correctly answered questions, 10 grains of rice will be donated to those in need.

Instructions:

  1. Download the FreeRice App!
  2. Join your Home Club's Team
  3. Answer Trivia questions to help end world hunger
  4. Record your scores under the service hour log via our linktree.
