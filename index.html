About Us

Key Club is so much more than just a normal club at your school. Along with all of the different service opportunities your home club provides, the Division is a way for members to make friends and be a part of something bigger than themselves. The division is in charge of planning many of the socials, fundraisers, and games that will occur throughout the year to make sure your Key Club journey is special! Division 04 East administers over numerous high schools including Beckman, Canyon, El Modena, Foothill, OCSA, Orange, Pacific Coast, Tustin, Villa Park, and Yorba Linda. Together in tandem with 7 other divisions around Orange County, we make up Rainbow Region 3. Under Region 3, we are a part of the California-Nevada-Hawaii District (Click on the left/top logo to access the CNH Cyberkey), the largest and most powerful district among Key Club International (Click on the right/bottom logo to access the Key Club International Website). Remember to stay updated with the Division Newsletters and the Division Instagram to find out more about our upcoming events. Keep on swimming, D04E!
