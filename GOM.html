Goldfish of the Month | October

“Canyon has done an amazing job this month. I have noticed them create amazing general slides and host creative ideas for their meetings for their members. “Rafael has shown tremendous growth in terms of professionalism and leadership throughout the term as president. Rafael continues to work hard and is making Foothill more of a stronger club by demonstrating his leadership skills.”

“Jonathan from Pacific Coast has demonstrated amazing qualities of leadership and dedication to service.
